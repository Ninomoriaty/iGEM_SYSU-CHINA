Assembly

We used pCMV-rtTA3(BBa_K3179102) and hEF1a-rtTA3(BBa_K3179102) to test the effects of different promoters on rtTA3 expression. The results are shown in the figure below.

Finally, we used LSBR5n3(BBa_K3179107) to measure the actual effective amount of miRNA in cells, assembled pTREL (BBA _ k317901) with pTRE-mi 663b-2kt-E1A-mir885-5p (BBA _ k3179105) or pTRE-mi 663b-2kt-E1A-E1B 55k-mir885-5p (BBA _ k3179106) to construct our target circuit, packaged into adenovirus, and constructed recombinant oncolytic adenovirus.
